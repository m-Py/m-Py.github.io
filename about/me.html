About me

I am Martin Papenberg, working as a post-doc researcher at the the department of Psychological Assessment and Differential Psychology at Heinrich Heine University Düsseldorf. I am interested in measurement, personality1 and programming2, among others.

Currently, I am primarily working on the R package anticlust package that enables the automatic selection of stimuli for psychological experiments. Check out my latest preprint on the anticlustering method: Using anticlustering to partition a stimulus pool into equivalent parts.
